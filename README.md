# 🧠 MindMate: Your AI-Powered Mental Health Companion

Welcome to **MindMate** — a full-stack digital platform built to support students' mental health with empathy, intelligence, and tech magic. Whether you're stressed before finals, feeling isolated, or just need a nudge to breathe, MindMate is your always-on emotional ally.

---

## 🚀 What’s Inside the MindMate Machine?

✨ **Sign-Up & Login**  
Secure, role-based access for students and counselors. JWT tokens keep your sessions safe and sound.

📓 **Mood Tracker + AI Feels Detector**  
Log your mood. Add a note. Let Hugging Face models decode your emotions in real time.  
> “Feeling anxious about exams?” → *Here’s a breathing exercise and a playlist to chill.*

🔍 **Smart Resource Recommender**  
Semantic search with Pinecone + Transformers. No keyword spam—just meaningful, mood-matching content.

🤝 **Peer Matching (Anonymous & Awesome)**  
AI clusters you with students feeling the same way. Share, vent, support—without revealing your identity.

🧑‍⚕️ **Counselor Booking (No Waitlists Here)**  
Browse counselors. Book instantly. Smart routing ensures you’re never stuck in a queue.

🎮 **Gamification + Study Buddy**  
Earn wellness badges. Get stress predictions from your academic calendar.  
> “Finals next week? Let’s prep your mind before your notes.”

🔐 **Privacy by Design**  
Encrypted everything. Role-based access. Embeddings instead of raw text. Your data = your space.

---

## 🧠 Next-Level Additions

💬 **Chatbot Integration**  
Talk to MindMate like a friend. Real-time responses, emotional support, and instant guidance.

🎙️ **Voice Emotion Detection** *(Coming Soon)*  
Speak your mind. MindMate listens—not just to words, but to how you say them.

Mental health in your pocket. Push notifications, offline access, and on-the-go support.

Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi... MindMate speaks your language.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Backend**: Flask (Python)
- **Databases**: MongoDB + Pinecone
- **AI/NLP**: Hugging Face Transformers, scikit-learn
- **Frontend**: React (optional)
- **Security**: JWT, bcrypt, Fernet encryption
- **Voice**: Google Speech API (planned)
